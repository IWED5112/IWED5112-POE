About Us

Founded in February 2025 by Realeboga Kgatshe,FORMA FITNESS was born out of a bold vision and passion for fitness and wellness. Realeboga Kgatshe grew up as an athlete that played academy soccer in Gauteng. After an injury he quit soccer and found a new passion in health and fitness. After 2 years of training alone, he recognized the need for a fitness community that goes beyond trend, one that prioirtizes progress and helping individuals become healthier versions of themselves.

With highly trained and educated experts and coaches, with a strong determination to change society's view on fitness, together our misssion is to empower individuals to become stronger, healthier, and more confident versions of themselves. At FORMA FITNESS we believe that fitness is a lifestyle, a mindset, and a commitment to progress.Join us in building a healthier society, one rep, one step and one transformation at a time.
